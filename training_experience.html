University of Life

Everywhere

I grew up in the countryside where I learned an appreciation for nature and hard work. I have now spent most of my working life in the city and am keen to get back somewhere nearer nature and the countryside which is one of the reasons I now live in Bath.

I have gained experience in many areas during my working life, not least in understanding and working with people from different cultures, both in Europe and Asia. Working in Asia was a formative experience becasue often there is not the company support network readily available and I had to rely on my own initiative to find the resources I needed and drive projects forward.
